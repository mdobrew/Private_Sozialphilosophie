**Targeted frontier reading (~83h Layer C + ~45h topic-conditional research), informed by working-group feedback.**

- **7.18 Honneth *Anerkennung* (2018) Full** (~21–25h additional) — typically fires (recognition is the modal Phase III topic).
- Any 2 of: 7.19 *Arbeitende Souverän* (~32–40h Light, work/democratic-Sittlichkeit); 8.12 *Fortschritt und Regression* (~28–36h Light, immanent critique); 7.10 Forst *Noumenal Republic* (~18–25h Light, power/justification); 14.9 Aglietta *Régulation* (~10h Orient → Light, capitalism-as-form).

**3-of-5 firing total: ~83h** at M41–M43. 4+ puts timeline at risk; 2 underpowers reviewer-coverage. Topic lock-in at M38 is the final gate.

**Feedback-conditional additions:** recent *WestEnd*/*DZfPh* issues; interlocutor-flagged monographs; Phase IV / Appendix C items the piece's argument now needs (e.g., Brenner if capitalism-as-social-form; Heller if radical needs; Mollenhauer if socialisation).

**W3.3 (Critical Review of a recent monograph, ~10h, ~1,500 words).** Run in parallel with the frontier reading and informal presentation. Pick a 2023–2025 monograph from a Frankfurt-tradition author — Forst, Jaeggi, Honneth, Stahl, Celikates, Allen, or a newer voice. Review structure per syllabus W3.3: (a) thesis, method, normative criterion; (b) internal-consistency evaluation; (c) one point where you could intervene constructively.
