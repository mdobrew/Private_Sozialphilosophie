### Why this module matters
This is the direct institutional and intellectual predecessor of the contemporary tradition. The key move you build here: combining Marx's critique of political economy with Weber's diagnosis of rationalisation, Lukács's theory of reification, and (in Adorno/Horkheimer) psychoanalytic theory to explain why emancipation failed — why Enlightenment reason dialectically produced domination. This module also establishes the distinctive *method* of Sozialphilosophie: immanent critique, ideology critique, diagnosis of social pathology. The W35 Horkheimer reconstruction is your second "what is critical theory?" foundational note — file it next to the Honneth "Pathologien" reconstruction from W5.
