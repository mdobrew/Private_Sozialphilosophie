## 6. Headline takeaway

**Structural floor for Phase III deliverables is intact through Layer 1+2+3-non-conflict (~155h cut, ~216 Recons).**

Per the layer-cascade audit §6: programme structural minimum sits at ~1,218h with ~50h headroom on M44 envelope. Phase III deliverables (Comparison Exercise + Craft Block + W3.2 + Tagung + journal submission) require: (i) Phase II MUST-Anchor coverage on the seven canonical authors — preserved through all non-conflict cuts; (ii) Comparison-essay-feeding tagged Recons (Module 7 + 8) — preserved.

**The Recon-count floor for credible Phase III is approximately 200 Recons** (the Comparison Exercise pulls from ~25 tagged Phase II Fulls; W3.2 draws on apparatus across ~50 Lights for reviewer-terrain coverage). All cuts up to and including **Layer 3 non-conflict (~216 Recons remaining, with 88 Full)** stay above this floor with margin.

**Two crossings of concern:**

1. **Layer 3 with interlocutor override** (208 Recons, 88F/120L) — the loss is *coherence* loss, not floor-crossing. v2.1 framework regression on 9.6 / 14.8 / 8.10 deletes the apparatus the bridge §2.4 interlocutor argument was upgraded *for*. Recon count is fine; the credibility hole at Q&A / scholar correspondence is the cost.

2. **8.2 *KvL* chapter-coverage demote (within Layer 3 non-conflict)** — the single largest Full delta (−5 Full). *KvL* remains Anchor by chs. 1–3 + 8–10, but the "10 Full Recons" deliverable promised in M8 weakens to "5 Full + 5 Light." This is the most visible deliverable-shape change in the cascade and the one most likely to surface in Jaeggi-modal reviewer Q&A as "you didn't engage chs. 4–7 at depth."

**Recommendation surface:** the Phase III Recon corpus survives Layer 1+2+3-non-conflict intact for production purposes. Layer 3-with-override is a coherence decision, not a Recon-count decision. Layers 4 + 5 buy timeline at zero Recon cost — preferable to Layer 3-override if interlocutor coherence is the binding constraint.
